Kundali Matching by Rajveer Patel – Ensure Compatibility for Marriage

Kundali or horoscope matching is an essential practice in Vedic astrology, particularly in Indian culture, to assess compatibility between two individuals, often for marriage. It involves comparing the birth charts (kundalis) of the prospective couple, which are created based on their exact time, date, and place of birth.

This analysis focuses on various aspects like Guna Milan (matching of qualities), doshas (afflictions), and planetary positions. The most critical part is the "Ashtakoota" system, which evaluates eight key factors, including emotional, physical, and mental compatibility.

A high match score is considered favorable for a harmonious relationship, while astrological remedies are often suggested to address any mismatches or doshas, ensuring balance and success in the partnership.
